Our Story

In October 2012, I decided it was time to take the plunge on go on a blind date set up by a friend. It was the day before Halloween and we met at a restaurant in Santa Barbara. The date was a disaster. I was late, he spilled the wine, and both our meals were wrong, but somehow he charmed me into a second date. And the rest is history…

The Proposal

After four years, he finally popped the question on the beach, during a beautiful spring day down the street from the same restaurant we had met at. He packed my favorite wine and a dinner he had made himself. It couldn’t have been more perfect.

Peirce Farm at Witch Hill

It’s an elegant 19th-century estate in historic Topsfield, Massachusetts. The Colonial Revival mansion has been artfully restored and features countless antiques and period furnishings, and an ornate fireplace. A quaint white-and-green barn hides a wonderful secret: a dramatic interior that’s certain to impress. Light streams in through high-set windows, filling the entire space with a romantic glow, and
the soaring, three-story ceiling is hung with a breathtaking modern candelabra chandelier. The house served as a haven for accused witches during the Salem Witch Trial, specifically Mary Towne Eastey, a direct ancestor of the bride's family.
